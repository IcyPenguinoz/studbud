Studbud


"A study session app"


Struggling to properly get your study sessions in check? Well Studbud - the study session web app is here to help! Studbud provides help in 3 main areas:


  • Task Management: in terms of managing your university tasks, whereas it be note-taking, finishing assignments or taking exams!

  • Time Management: in terms of helping to get focused on doing those tasks - with the help of the simple timer and the Pomodoro timer!

  • Content Management: in terms of using simple yet effective acronym maker in helping to properly condense many terms and concepts into simple abbreviations!

This is where you can manage your tasks via adding your own tasks and detailing them alongside being able to organise them further by using the "Kanban Board" feature!
